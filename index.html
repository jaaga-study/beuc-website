HOW TO GET ONE

Electric Unicycles are not yet commercially available in India. They are made and sold in China and getting one here involves a number of steps including sending money to China, shipping large Lithium batteries, and going through customs. Generally the battery needs to be separated from the unicycle for shipping and needs to be reassembled on receipt.

EUC-Club Members

Ask a club member on how to get one. Generally, club members work with a club pro who handles money transfer, shipping, customs, assembly and ongoing services.

Ali Baba

Visit Alibaba and Buy yourself one from there.
